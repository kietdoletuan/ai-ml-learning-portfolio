## Data Visualization — Lesson Projects

**Project 1: Titanic Survival Correlation Analysis**
Explores which passenger features correlate most strongly with survival.
Correlation heatmap across survival, age, class, sex, fare, and family
size. Supported by survival count by sex, age distribution, and fare
distribution of survivors.

**Project 2: Weather Data — Interpolation vs Drop NaN**
Side-by-side comparison of two missing value strategies on temperature
time-series data. Same charts built twice — once with dropna, once with
time-based interpolation — to visualise the impact of each approach on
the output.

**Data**
-Titanic: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/yasserh/titanic-dataset
-Weather: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/budincsevity/szeged-weather FILTERED OUT ONLY TEMPERATURE AND DATE
